Mess FAQs

Q.1 How and when should I make payments for the mess facilities?
Answer:

By using the online website of your campus you can pay the mess fees at the start of every semester.

Q.2 Can I bring guests or visitors to the mess?
Answer:

Yes, you can bring parents or any guests but for them, you need to pay charges at the entry of the mess.

Q.3 What are the operating hours of the dining hall?
Answer:

Breakfast 7:30 to 10:00 AM; Lunch 12:30 to 2:15 PM; Dinner 8:00 to 10:30 PM and Snacks 5:30 to 6:30 PM.

Q.4 Is there a process for providing feedback or suggestions about the mess facilities?
Answer:

Yes, they collect feedback and suggestions by floating a Google form.

Q.5 Are there different cuisines or options for vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals?
Answer:

Yes, there will be different cuisines for vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals.

Q.6 Are there CCTV cameras installed in common areas?
Answer:

Yes, there are CCTV cameras near the stairs and near the parking area and at the entrance of every hostel.

Q.7 Are there dedicated security guards?
Answer:

Yes, there is a minimum of two security guards for every building in the campus.

Q.8 Are there any restrictions on guest access?
Answer:

Guests should not go into the hostels of students.
